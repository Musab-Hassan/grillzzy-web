Who we Are

Originating from the Okanagan valley, Grillzzy was born as a vision of flavour. A dream to blend a thousand generations of flavours from three different civilizations. Until it happened and it was a dream no more. The perfect blend of Central Asia, South Asia, and Canadian flavour merged beautifully into one recipe. That recipe is the recipe we promise with every pack of our signature kebbabs.
